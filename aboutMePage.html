NASAM Certified Personal Trainer

 

 

What I Do

I’m a personal trainer and online coach based out of Cityfitness Thorndon. I love to help people achieve their goals and change their lives. It is a privilege to be able to share my passion, as well as pass on all my knowledge and experience I have picked up over the years. Alongside my certification with the National Association of Sports Medicine I have had years of personal experience with experimenting with my own training. With a background in sports performance and bodybuilding, I work with a variety of clients from athletes to people just trying to be the best possible version of themselves.

Something else about me

I got into the gym to develop my power, strength and physique for rugby. This pushed me to become as well rounded of an athlete as possible. Exposing myself to different areas of the fitness industry from hypertrophy, powerlifting, fat loss and bodybuilding. After a season as a ski instructor in Japan, I found my love for teaching. Combining my love for the gym and teaching, becoming a personal trainer was the next step for me.
